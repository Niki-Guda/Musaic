Meet Smith World!!

dynamic hip-hop choreographer who has taken the South African hip-hop scene by storm. Known for his innovative dance moves and electrifying performances, Smith has traveled the world showcasing his talent and has worked on major projects, including the popular “Step Up” movie series. His journey from local dance battles to international stages is a testament to his dedication and passion for hip-hop. Smith’s rise to fame began in the vibrant streets of Johannesburg, where his unique style and creativity quickly set him apart. His choreography blends traditional African dance elements with contemporary hip-hop, creating a fresh and captivating experience for audiences. His debut performance at the South African Hip Hop Awards earned him critical acclaim and solidified his reputation as a leading choreographer.

Throughout his career, Smith has collaborated with numerous international artists and choreographed for high-profile music videos and films. His work on the “Step Up” movies showcased his ability to create visually stunning and technically challenging dance routines, earning him a global fanbase. Known for his dynamic energy and innovative choreography, Smith’s performances are always a highlight, whether on stage or screen. As a trailblazer in the hip-hop dance community, Smith World continues to inspire and mentor young dancers. His commitment to his craft and his community has solidified his reputation as not just a choreographer, but a cultural ambassador for South African hip-hop.
