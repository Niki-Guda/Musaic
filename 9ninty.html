Meet 9Ninty!!

Emerging from the gritty streets of Johannesburg’s CBD, 9Ninty is a rising star in the trap music scene. Known for his hard-hitting beats and raw, authentic lyrics, his music captures the essence of urban life in Jozi. 9Ninty’s debut mixtape, “City Lights,” showcases his ability to blend intense trap rhythms with poignant storytelling, reflecting the struggles and triumphs of his community. His journey began in the underground clubs of Johannesburg, where his electrifying performances quickly garnered attention. His breakout single, “CBD Dreams,” became an anthem for the city’s youth, resonating with fans who relate to his powerful narratives and relentless energy. With a unique style that combines traditional African influences with modern trap elements, 9Ninty is redefining the genre.

On stage, 9Ninty is a force to be reckoned with. His dynamic presence and high-energy performances leave audiences captivated and craving more. Whether he’s headlining major festivals or performing at local venues, 9Ninty’s shows are an unforgettable experience, filled with passion and intensity. Dedicated to his craft and his roots, 9Ninty continues to push the boundaries of trap music. His commitment to authenticity and his community has solidified his reputation as not just an artist, but a voice for the streets of Johannesburg.
