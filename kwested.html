WHAT TO EXPECT

Nestled in the heart of Mohakeng, a small township in the Westrand region of Gauteng, Kwested Markets is a bi-weekly event that has become a cornerstone of the local community. Every two weeks, the market transforms into a bustling hub of activity, showcasing the rich cultural tapestry of the area. Kwested Markets is renowned for its vibrant atmosphere, where local creatives take center stage. From musicians and dancers to poets and visual artists, the market provides a platform for these talents to shine. Visitors are treated to live performances that capture the essence of Mohakeng’s artistic spirit, making each visit a unique experience.

In addition to the artistic performances, Kwested Markets is a haven for local brands and small businesses. Vendors offer a diverse array of products, from handmade crafts and fashion items to delicious local delicacies. This not only supports the local economy but also allows visitors to discover and purchase unique, high-quality goods directly from the creators. More than just a market, Kwested Markets is a community gathering that fosters a sense of togetherness and pride among the residents of Mohakeng. It is a place where people come to connect, celebrate, and support one another. The market’s lively atmosphere, filled with the sounds of music and the vibrant colors of local crafts, creates a welcoming environment for all.

By providing a platform for local artists and businesses, it plays a crucial role in nurturing and showcasing the township’s unique identity. Whether you’re a local resident or a visitor, Kwested Markets offers an enriching experience that highlights the best of what Mohakeng has to offer.
