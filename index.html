WHAT WE DO

There's plenty of talent to keep the world vibrant and worth living in. Our goal is to take all this talent and bring it to the people. Think of us as your one-stop-shop to all your favourite artist news, and a glimps into who your new favourite artist is going to be. We make it our business to seek out fresh new talent on the rise and put them on display for all to see. We do of course have our "own" event called WeSession where a handfull of artists come together to make a fresh piece of music through sessioning. We present you with current news in the creative space to keep you updated and moving along with us.



WHAT YOU CAN DO FOR US

  1. Feel free to tag us on your socials regarding our events or any interests related to us.
  2. Write to us through this website or dm our social media about any sugestions or an artists you would like to see on our platforms.
  3. Be on the lookout for upcoming events!!! We truly want to see you there.
  4. Tell your friend/family who's an artist to reach out to us, we'd love to connect with new talent
