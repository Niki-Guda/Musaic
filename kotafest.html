SOWETO KOTAFEST

WHAT TO EXPECT

Get ready for a feast of flavors and a celebration of culture at the Soweto Kota Fest 2024! Taking place in the heart of Soweto, this vibrant event brings together food enthusiasts, local vendors, and entertainment for an unforgettable experience. Indulge in a wide array of kota variations, enjoy live performances from local artists, and explore cultural exhibitions that celebrate Soweto’s rich heritage. With family-friendly activities, local crafts and goods, and a festive atmosphere, this event promises fun for all ages and a true sense of community spirit. Whether you’re a foodie, a culture enthusiast, or simply looking for a fun day out, Soweto Kota Fest 2024 is the place to be. Don’t miss out on this celebration of one of South Africa’s most beloved street foods and the vibrant culture of Soweto!

  • 07 September 2024
