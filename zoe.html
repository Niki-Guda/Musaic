Meet Zoe!!

This skilled songtress, born and raised in the heart of Jozi carries a light and beautiful tone reminiscent of the sweet tones of maskandi singers. Zoe started off playing the guitar and busking in the streets of the CBD, she was then discovered by vocal coach, Rebecca Smith, who took her in and build her vocal ability which she now uses to serenade the masses.

With her latest EP, Soular, Zoe speaks to the very soul of the people. She recently debuted this work of art at the State theatre in arch of 2024. Want to work with Benta? Book her now:
